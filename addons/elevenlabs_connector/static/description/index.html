Why ElevenLabs Voice Connector?

Give your team the power to query Odoo with their voice — no typing, no navigation, instant answers.

ElevenLabs Voice Connector bridges the gap between natural speech and your Odoo database. Powered by ElevenLabs' state-of-the-art speech-to-text API and your choice of OpenAI or Google Gemini, users can ask questions out loud and receive accurate, real-time answers pulled directly from live Odoo data.

Built natively inside Odoo 19 using the OWL framework, the assistant lives as an in-app widget — no browser plugins, no external dashboards. API keys are configured once in Settings and immediately available across your entire Odoo instance.

Every conversation is saved in Odoo's database under the Voice Assistant menu, giving managers full visibility into what the team is asking and ensuring no insight is ever lost. Developed and supported by Dow Group, certified Odoo Gold Partner — www.dowgroup.com

How It Works

Five steps from voice to answer.

1

Record

Click the microphone button in the Voice Assistant widget.
2

Transcribe

Your voice is sent to ElevenLabs STT for accurate speech-to-text conversion.
3

Process

Text is sent to OpenAI or Gemini for natural language understanding.
4

Query

AI automatically queries your live Odoo database based on the question.
5

Display

The answer appears instantly in Odoo and is saved to conversation history.

Installation & Setup

Up and running in minutes.

1

Install

Install from Odoo App Store or upload the module to your instance.
2

Settings

Go to Settings → scroll to "ElevenLabs Voice Connector" section.
3

Configure

Enter your ElevenLabs API key, select AI provider, enter its API key. Click "Test Connection".
4

Start

Go to Voice Assistant → Start Voice Assistant and click the microphone!

System Requirements

Odoo 19.0 or later, tested on Odoo.sh
Python requests Standard in Odoo — no additional installation needed
Internet Connection Required for API calls to ElevenLabs and AI providers
