Dojo Management

Professional Martial Arts School Administration for Odoo 19

Belt Ranking System Interactive Dashboard Performance Analytics Multi-Style Support

Overview

Dojo Management is the most comprehensive Odoo 19 module for martial arts schools. Manage students, track belt progressions, monitor attendance, handle payments, and gain powerful insights through interactive dashboards and analytics. Built for dojos that demand excellence.

Key Features

Student Management

Complete profiles with photos, emergency contacts, medical info, and performance tracking.

Belt Rankings

Track progression from White to Black Belt (Dan levels). Automated promotion wizard included.

Interactive Dashboard

Real-time analytics, charts, revenue tracking, attendance rates, and KPI monitoring.

Multi-Style Support

Supports Karate, Judo, BJJ, Taekwondo, Kung Fu, Muay Thai, and more concurrently.

Session Scheduling

Organize classes, assign instructors, manage capacity, and track session history.

Finance & Payments

Track tuition, manage due dates, detect late payments, and analyze revenue streams.

Screenshots

Experience the power of real-time dojo analytics.
